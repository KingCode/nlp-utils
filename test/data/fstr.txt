L.B. Foster Company (Nasdaq: FSTR)
announced today that its Board of Directors has authorized a $0.03 per share
regular quarterly cash dividend on its issued and outstanding shares of common
stock which represents a 20% increase over the prior year quarterly dividend
paid. The dividend is payable Friday, March 29, 2013 to shareholders of
record at the close of business on March 15, 2013.
