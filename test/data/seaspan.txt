In March 2013, Seaspan's board of directors approved a 25.0% increase in
the quarterly common share dividend to $0.3125 per share, which dividend will
be subsequently declared for the quarter ending March 31, 2013. This $0.0625
per share increase to Seaspan's quarterly common share dividend represents
the fourth increase since March 31, 2010 for an aggregate increase of 212.5%.
Seaspan expects common share dividends for the four quarters ending December
31, 2013 to total $1.25 per share.
