Hancock Holding Company (Nasdaq: HBHC [FREE Stock Trend Analysis]) today announced that
the company's board of directors approved a regular first quarter 2013 common stock cash dividend of
$0.24 per share.

Approved during the January meeting of the company's board of directors, the regular quarterly
common stock cash dividend is payable March 15, 2013 to shareholders of record as of March 5, 2013.
