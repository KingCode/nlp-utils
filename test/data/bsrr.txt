Sierra Bancorp (Nasdaq: BSRR [FREE Stock Trend Analysis]), parent of Bank of the Sierra, today
announced that its Board of Directors has declared a regular quarterly cash dividend of $0.06 per share,
subsequent to the Board's review of the Company's financial results and capital strength for the quarter
ended December 31, 2012. The dividend will be paid on February 14, 2013 to shareholders of record as
of January 31, 2013.
